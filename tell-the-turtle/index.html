Tell the turtle

Published on 2014-09-26

I just released a pet project I’ve been working on for the past couple of days. Say hello to “Tell the turtle”.

The concept is simple. You get a random topic to write about. Submit your work to the pool and in return, you receive a random work from the pool after some random hours.

I wrote the web server and mailer in Go. I store everything in Redis and Mailgun sends the emails. I’ve rekindled my love for Go. It’s such a fun language to write in.
